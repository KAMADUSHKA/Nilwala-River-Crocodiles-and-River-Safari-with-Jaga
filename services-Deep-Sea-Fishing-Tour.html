Deep Sea Fishing Tour – Offshore Adventure Near Matara

  • Head south from Mirissa to the legendary waters off Dondra Point—the southernmost tip of Sri Lanka, known for its rich marine life and deep fishing grounds. This is where real anglers go to chase the giants of the sea.
  • From powerful tuna to swift mahi-mahi and even the elusive sailfish, every moment offshore holds the promise of the ultimate catch. Guided by expert local fishermen who know these waters like their own backyard, you’ll venture far into the deep blue using pro-grade fishing gear and proven techniques.

This is more than a tour—it's a test of skill, strength, and ocean intuition. Out here, the sea rewards the bold.
