Explore the Wild Side of Nilwala River in Matara

A tour unlike any other—peaceful yet thrilling, quiet yet unforgettable.

Low Deep Sea Fishing Tour – Gentle Fishing Near Weligama Bay

  • Just a short ride from Mirissa, Weligama Bay offers the perfect setting for a light, family-friendly fishing experience. Calm seas, close-to-shore reefs, and a variety of smaller fish make this tour ideal for beginners and casual adventurers
  • Drift over turquoise waters as your guide helps you catch reef fish like snapper, mackerel, and emperor. No stress, no heavy swells—just good vibes, salty air, and the simple joy of fishing Sri Lankan-style.

Peaceful waters. Easy catches. Perfect for all ages.
