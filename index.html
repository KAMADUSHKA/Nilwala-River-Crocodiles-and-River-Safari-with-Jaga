Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our crocodile-spotting river safaris and what to expect during your adventure with Jaga.

How long does the river safari take?

Our safaris usually last 2 to 2.5 hours, depending on river conditions and wildlife sightings.

Is it safe to see crocodiles up close?

Yes, your guide Jaga is experienced and follows strict safety measures. You’ll observe crocodiles safely from the boat.

What time is best for the safari?

The best times are early morning or late afternoon, when crocodiles and other wildlife are most active.

Do I need to book in advance?

Not Mandatory. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially during tourist season.

What should I bring for the tour?

Bring a hat, sunscreen, camera, and water. We provide life jackets and everything else you need for the safari.
